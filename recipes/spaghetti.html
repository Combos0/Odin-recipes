Spaghetti!

Spaghetti is a great dish that can be served by itself or as a side with another entree, like chicken parmesan.

Ingredients

  • Choice of spaghetti noodle
  • Tomato sauce
  • Parmesan cheese
  • salt

Recipe

  1. Bring salted water to a boil.
  2. Cook spaghetti for around ten minutes or until limp.
  3. heat choice of sauce.
  4. mix sauce and noodles.
  5. Let stand for five minutes then mix again.
  6. Sprinkle cheese on top.
  7. Serve and enjoy!
