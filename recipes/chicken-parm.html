Chicken Parmesan!

Chicken parmesan is my guilty pleasure food. It's not very good for you, but it is worth the pain.

This recipe is fairly straight forward, but can go wrong if any part is missed or skipped.

Chicken parmesan also goes great when paired with my spaghetti recipe!

Ingredients

  • Boneless Chicken Breast
  • Parmesan
  • Mozzarella
  • Provolone
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Large Eggs
  • Panko Bread Crumbs
  • Flour
  • Oil

Recipe

  1. Tenderize chicken breast into thin cutlets.
  2. Sprinkle flour on smashed chicken.
  3. Dip chicken in eggs.
  4. Dip eggy chicken in Panko.
  5. Repeat steps two and three so that the chicken is double breaded.
  6. Flash cook the chicken in the oil over high heat for around two minutes.
  7. Put the tomato sauce on top of the chicken, then the cheese.
  8. Bake in the oven at 450degrees fahrenheit till the internal temperature reads 165degrees fahrenheit.
  9. Rest for ten to fifteen minutes then enjoy!
