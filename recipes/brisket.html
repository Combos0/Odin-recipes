BBQ Brisket!

BBQ beef brisket is one of my favorite meals to cook! the process itself takes many hours and can go wrong instantly, but its worth learning to make i the end.

The recipe requires access to a BBQ smoker, a good choice of wood and dry rub, butcher paper, a dry cooler, and of course a nice cut of brisket.

Most of the work is going to be done upfront, at the beginning of the cook. Lets get started!

Required ingredients/utensils

  • Brisket
  • Smoker
  • BBQ Rub
  • Butcher Paper
  • Dry Cooler
  • Time and Patience

Recipe Steps

  1. Prep the brisket with a generous amount of your BBQ rub
  2. Rest for thirty minutes to one hour
  3. Place brisket fat side down on smoker at 200degrees fahrenheit
  4. Close lid and smoke for one and a half to two hours per pound of meat
  5. Take brisket off smoker and wrap in butcher paper
  6. Return brisket to smoker at 250degrees fahrenheit
  7. Cook till 180degrees fahrenheit internal temperature
  8. Take off smoker and place in dry cooler for two to three hours
  9. Slice and enjoy
